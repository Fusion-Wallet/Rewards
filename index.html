MainNet Token Swap

Important Information

ONLY send ERC20 FSN tokens to the token swap address from a wallet to which you hold the private key.

Do NOT send ERC20 FSN tokens to the tokens swap address directly from an exchange.

The Ethereum key pair used to swap your ECR-20 FSN Tokens is the same key pair you must use to access your Native FSN Tokens using whallet.net.

How to Swap

Important Information

Send your ERC20 FSN from your Ethereum wallet to which you hold the private key to the swap address provided below.

Your ERC20 FSN is converted to MainNet FSN. *This process may take 10+ minutes.

Open your wallet on whallet.net with the same key pair used to send ERC20 FSN from your Ethereum wallet in step 1, your MainNet FSN will be in your wallet!
